# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone participating in this project, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, sexual identity, or sexual orientation, will be treated with respect and dignity.

---

## Our Values

We believe in:
- **Inclusivity**: Everyone is welcome and valued
- **Respect**: We listen and learn from each other
- **Transparency**: We communicate openly and honestly
- **Collaboration**: We work together toward common goals
- **Excellence**: We strive to produce quality work

---

## Expected Behavior

All participants are expected to:

### Be Respectful
- Treat everyone with kindness and respect
- Assume good intent; ask for clarification if confused
- Respect diverse viewpoints and experiences
- Use inclusive language

### Be Professional
- Provide constructive feedback focused on code and ideas, not people
- Welcome newcomers and mentor them
- Share knowledge and help others learn
- Acknowledge others' contributions

### Be Collaborative
- Work together to resolve conflicts
- Give credit where it's due
- Participate in good faith
- Support the growth of the community

### Report Issues Responsibly
- Report security vulnerabilities privately (see SECURITY.md)
- Use issue tracker for bugs and features
- Provide context and details in reports

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following are considered unacceptable and will not be tolerated:

### Harassment & Discrimination
- Offensive comments related to personal characteristics
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Deliberate intimidation or threats
- Harassment based on identity or characteristics
- Stalking or doxxing

### Disruptive Behavior
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Spam or excessive self-promotion
- Deliberate disruption of discussions
- Off-topic or inflammatory posts
- Spreading misinformation

### Privacy Violations
- Sharing others' private information without consent
- Posting personal details (email, address, etc.)
- Using someone's identity without permission
- Recording or photographing without consent

### Hostile Conduct
- Aggressive or hostile language
- Personal attacks
- Exclusionary behavior
- Any form of abuse

---

## Reporting & Enforcement

### How to Report
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

1. **Report to maintainers**: Email tushardharaster@gmail.com
2. **Be specific**: Include details, context, and screenshots if applicable
3. **Stay safe**: Don't engage with the person if it feels unsafe

### What Happens Next
1. **Investigation**: Maintainers will review the report (24-72 hours)
2. **Response**: We'll follow up with you about actions taken
3. **Action**: Depending on severity:
   - Warning or conversation
   - Temporary mute or suspension
   - Permanent ban

### Confidentiality
- Reports are confidential
- We won't publicly name reporters
- We will keep you informed of outcomes
- We will respect victim privacy
